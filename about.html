Our Foundation

What We Believe

The Bible

God's written word, given by divine inspiration. The infallible revelation of His will.

The Trinity

One God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, a unity of three co-eternal Persons.

Salvation

Through Christ alone, we are justified, adopted, and made heirs to eternal life.

The Sabbath

The seventh day is the Sabbath of the Lord, a sign of our loyalty to Him.

Second Coming

The blessed hope of the church, the grand climax of the gospel.

Christian Behavior

We are called to be a godly people who think, feel, and act in harmony with heaven.

What should I wear?

What about my kids?

We have engaging Sabbath School classes for all ages starting at 8:30 AM.

Our main Divine Service runs from 10:30 AM to roughly 12:30 PM.
