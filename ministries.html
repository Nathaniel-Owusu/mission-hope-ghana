Every Member a Minister

At Mission Hope, we believe that everyone has something unique to offer. Our ministries are designed to serve our church family and reach out to our community. Whether you love teaching, singing, helping those in need, or organizing events, there is a place for you here.

How to Join a Ministry

1

Pray & Reflect

Consider your talents and passions. Where do you feel God leading you to serve? Take time to pray for guidance.

2

Connect with a Leader

Reach out to the ministry leader or a church elder. They would love to answer your questions and welcome you.

3

Start Serving

Attend the next meeting or event. Don't worry about being perfect; just come with a willing heart.
